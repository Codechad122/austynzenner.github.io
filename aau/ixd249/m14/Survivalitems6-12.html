Survival Items needed for a gaming convention

If you get an open cut or any type of open wound having antibiotics can keep you safe.

Having some Id will help you get through almost any type of line in the convention.

Having a water bottle to drink from will help you save money and time when trying to find or get more water.

Having some painkillers when your body is aching or is in pain will help a lot.

Having an extra pair of shoes will let you keep your first pair in shape and dry as most of these gaming conventions are extremly hot and stuffy sometimes.

Having your phone charger is a must since you will need it to order rides and make calls.
