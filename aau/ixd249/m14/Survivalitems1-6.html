Survival Items needed for a gaming convention

Make sure to take a few snacks this will help you reduce the amount of time you will spend looking for food.

Having an extra small bag will help take the strain off your back and help you to carry more stuff.

Having your computer charger will let you recharge and keep playing those demos without having to wait in line.

Having some extra cash can let you buy things you really need in case there is an emergency.

Bringing your own coffe cup will help you skip all the lines waiting to get a cup for drinks.

Bringing your own laptop will let you play all of the new demos that are coming out without having to wait in line.
