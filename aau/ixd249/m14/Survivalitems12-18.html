Survival Items needed for a gaming convention

Having a guidebook will be able to help you answer any questions you might have.

Having your hotel room booked before you arrive will let you plan more accordingly.

Having your backpack will let you be able to carry more cool free stuff you will receive.

Having a map of the convention will help you plan more accordingly.

Having a lanyard will let you keep your ID out so you can just flash your card and get in faster.

Having water is vital since you will be walking all day you will be thirsty.
