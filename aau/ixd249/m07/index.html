How To Cut A Ferrets Nails

Make sure you have ferret or cat nail clippers, salmon oil, or ferretone and make sure you have styptic powder or corn starch in case you cut the blood vessel in the nail so you can stop the bleeding really quick.

Each Ferret is different some like ferretone and some like salmon oil. Ferrets can nuts for both of these this can make it easier to cut the nails as it will keep them distracted. Or you can get another to help you scruff them while you cut the nails.

Before you begin cutting or doing anything make sure you take a look and see if the nail is healthy if it is soft and green do not cut go to your vet and have them take care of it. green and soft means there is a bacteria infection and cutting it could make it worse.

When you begin cutting you will want to push very gently on the ferrets paw and push the nails out so you can see where the blood vessel is. Make sure you do this a very bright lit room as if it is dark iyt will be very hard to see the blood vessel and an accident could occur.
